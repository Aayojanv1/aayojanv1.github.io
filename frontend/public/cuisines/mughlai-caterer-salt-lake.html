The Mughlai menu

Mughlai signature dishes.

A complete Mughlai spread typically pairs 2-3 starters (kebab), 2 mains (korma + biryani), 2 breads (sheermal + naan), and a sweet (shahi tukda or phirni).

🍢
Galouti Kebab
Lucknow's softest minced-mutton kebab. Melts at first bite. The signature opener.
🌶️
Tunday Kebab
Lucknowi mutton kebab — 60+ spices, slow-cooked. Iconic on Mughlai menus.
🍗
Murgh Malai Tikka
Creamy chicken kebab with mild cardamom. Crowd-friendly tandoor centrepiece.
🍖
Mutton Korma
Royal Awadhi gravy — cream, cashew, saffron. Slow-cooked, never quick.
🐔
Chicken Changezi
Old-Delhi style chicken — tomato-cream gravy, layered spices, deep finish.
🍞
Sheermal · Naan · Roomali
Sheermal is the saffron-milk flatbread that defines Mughlai. Naan and roomali for crowd-friendly pairing.
🍛
Lucknowi Biryani
Mughlai-style biryani — aromatic, mildly spiced, layered. Different from Kolkata-style.
🍮
Shahi Tukda · Phirni
Shahi tukda — saffron bread pudding. Phirni — chilled rice-milk dessert. Mughlai finish.
