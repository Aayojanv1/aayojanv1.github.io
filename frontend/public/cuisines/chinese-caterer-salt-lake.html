The Calcutta-Chinese menu

What's on a Chinese spread?

A complete Chinese spread typically pairs 2-3 starters (dim sum, wonton), 2 mains (chilli chicken + manchurian), 2 noodles/rice, 1 sweet.

🥟
Steamed Momos · Dim Sum
Chicken, prawn, mutton, vegetable. Steamed in bamboo baskets, served with red chilli sauce.
🍤
Crab Wonton · Crispy Prawn
Crispy wonton with crab filling. Honey-glazed prawns. Cocktail-hour starters.
🌶️
Chilli Chicken
The Calcutta-Chinese signature. Dry, bold, soy-rich. The dish nobody refuses.
🌿
Chilli Paneer / Manchurian
Pure-veg Chinese mains. Manchurian gravy or dry. Bell pepper, scallion, soy.
🍜
Hakka Noodles · Chow Mein
Loose, wok-fired, slightly oily. Egg, chicken, vegetable. The Calcutta way.
🍚
Schezwan / Garlic Fried Rice
Wok-fired rice. Schezwan spicy, garlic mild, mixed-veg crowd-friendly.
🦞
Honey Chicken · Chilli Fish
Sweet-spicy honey chicken. Chilli fish (bhetki or basa) in soy-pepper. Wedding-grade.
🍮
Date Pancake · Honey Noodle
Date pancake the classic Calcutta-Chinese dessert. Honey noodle ice cream for parties.

Chinese FAQs

Questions Salt Lake hosts ask.

How much does Chinese catering cost?+

₹350-₹450/plate standard. Full Calcutta-Chinese ₹450-₹600/plate. Live wok ₹650+/plate.

What's Calcutta-Chinese?+

Indo-Chinese fusion from Tangra's Hakka community — bolder, soy-rich, spicier than mainland Chinese. Chilli Chicken, Chow Mein, Manchurian originated or refined here.

Live wok counter?+

Yes — chef-operated wok station. Stir-fries to order. ₹100-₹150/plate add-on.

Dim sum and momos?+

Yes — steamed momos, wontons, dim sum baskets, fried wontons. Chicken, prawn, mutton, veg.

Vegetarian Chinese full menu?+

Yes — Chilli Paneer, Vegetable Manchurian, Schezwan Veg Rice, Mushroom Wok, Veg Hakka Noodles, Veg Momos.

Common events?+

Birthday parties, corporate events, mehndi-day, wedding Chinese counters, sangeet.
