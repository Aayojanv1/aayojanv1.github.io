The Continental menu

What's a proper Continental spread?

Cocktail-dinner format. Stations + plated rounds + dessert spread. Partners can match Indian-Continental or pure-Continental depending on guest crowd.

🍝
Live Pasta Station
Penne, fettuccine, ravioli. Guests pick sauce — Arrabiata, Alfredo, pesto. Chef plates fresh.
🥩
Sizzler / Steak Counter
Lamb steak, chicken, fish, paneer. Chef-flamed on iron plates at the counter.
🍗
Roast Chicken / Pork
Whole roast, carving station, jus + chimichurri. Premium counter for milestone events.
🥗
Salad Bar
Caesar, Greek, Russian, beetroot-quinoa. Made fresh, dressed at the station.
🍞
Bruschetta · Crostini
Tomato-basil, mushroom-truffle, olive tapenade. Cocktail-pacing appetisers.
🧀
Cheese Fondue · Cheese Board
Hot cheese fondue with breads/veg. Premium cheese board — brie, gouda, blue, crackers.
🍲
Soup Bar
Minestrone, cream of mushroom, broccoli-almond. Hot ladles, fresh garnish.
🍰
Dessert Station
Tiramisu, chocolate fondant, panna cotta, crepe station, ice cream bar.

Continental FAQs

Questions Newtown hosts ask.

How much does Continental catering cost?+

₹500-₹650/plate mid-range. ₹700-₹900/plate premium. Cocktail dinner format ₹900+/plate.

What's on a Continental menu?+

Pasta, grilled chicken/fish, lamb roast, steaks, salads (Caesar, Greek, Russian), bruschetta, fondue, soup, dessert (tiramisu, fondant, panna cotta).

Live pasta and sizzler counters?+

Yes — signature Newtown Continental wedding add-ons. Pasta ₹100-₹150/plate, sizzler ₹150-₹200/plate.

Vegetarian options?+

Yes — mushroom pasta, vegetable lasagna, paneer steaks, grilled vegetable platters, ratatouille, fondue. ₹400-₹600/plate.

Common events for Continental?+

Sangeet cocktail dinners, mehndi-day, reception Continental section (alongside Bengali main), milestone birthdays, corporate gala dinners.

How early to book?+

Standard 2 weeks. Wedding-week cocktail: 4-6 weeks. Peak Nov-Feb weddings: 8 weeks.

Need Continental catering in Newtown?

Event, batch, station preferences (pasta / sizzler / roast / fondue) — tell us. Quotes in hours.
