Biryani FAQs

Questions biryani buyers ask.

How much does biryani catering cost?+

Chicken ₹350-₹450/plate. Mutton ₹450-₹650/plate. Veg ₹280-₹380/plate. Add-ons (firni, kebab, raita) usually bundled.

What's special about Kolkata-style biryani?+

Milder, aromatic, with signature boiled potato (aloo) and boiled egg (dim) on every plate. Long-grain basmati, slow-cooked meat, layered masala.

Biryani for 25 guests at home?+

Yes — 25 plates minimum. Delivered in handi or individual boxes. Includes aloo, dim, salad, raita.

Live biryani counter for weddings?+

Yes — live dum, chef-plated, paired with kebab counter. Add-on ₹80-₹150/plate over standard.

Veg / Jain options?+

Yes — Subz biryani, Paneer biryani, Jain biryani (no onion-garlic-potato). ₹280-₹380/plate.

How early to book?+

25-50 plates: 3-5 days. Weddings 200+: 3-4 weeks. Peak Nov-Feb weddings: 6-8 weeks.
