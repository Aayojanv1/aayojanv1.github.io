Multi-cuisine FAQs

Questions multi-cuisine hosts ask.

How much does multi-cuisine cost?+

2-cuisine ₹500-₹650/plate. 3-4 cuisine ₹700-₹900/plate. Premium ₹1000+/plate.

How does it work logistically?+

Partner kitchens divide by cuisine section — separate prep teams. At venue, each cuisine has its own buffet station or counter.

Why multi-cuisine vs single?+

Mixed-preference crowds — Bengali for elders, Continental for younger, Chinese for kids, Mughlai for bold eaters. Common at receptions.

How many cuisines combined?+

3-4 typical. Each additional cuisine adds ₹80-₹150/plate.

Live counters across cuisines?+

Yes — Bengali fish-fry, Mughlai tandoor, Continental pasta, Chinese wok, dessert station. 4-6 counters common.

Minimum guest count?+

100+ recommended. Smaller events: 1-2 cuisine spreads more efficient.
