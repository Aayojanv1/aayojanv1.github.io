Bengali Caterer FAQs

Questions Newtown Bengalis ask.

How much does Bengali catering cost?+

₹300-₹450/plate home-style. ₹450-₹600/plate ceremony spread. ₹600-₹700+/plate premium wedding (Hilsa, Chingri Malaikari, Paturi).

What signature dishes are available?+

Doi Ilish, Kosha Mangsho, Mochar Ghonto, Sukto, Bhetki Paturi, Doi Maach, Rohu Kalia, Cholar Dal, Niramish Aloo Dum, Basanti Pulao, Luchi, Mishti Doi, Payesh, Rasgulla, Sandesh.

Are partner kitchens Bengali-owned?+

Yes — Karabi Dey, Sayan Banerjee, Sandip Sarkar. All home-trained in Bengali cooking traditions.

Can I get pure-veg (niramish)?+

Yes — niramish (no onion, no garlic) menus for puja days, Saraswati Puja, Lakshmi Puja, Annaprasan.

Is Hilsa available?+

Yes — Hilsa Kalia, Bhapa Ilish, Doi Ilish. Seasonal pricing applies; +₹150-₹300/plate over standard.

Minimum batch?+

25 plates. Scales from 25-50 home parties to 200-800 wedding spreads.
