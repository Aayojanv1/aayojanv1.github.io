The story

A kitchen with one truth.

Curry-কথা — literally "Curry Tales" — is a Bengali kitchen that tells the truth on every plate. No multi-cuisine fusion, no compromise menus. Sayan Banerjee cooks the way Bengali grandmothers cooked: slow, honest, recognisable.

And the pricing reflects that philosophy. From a simple biye barir bhog at ₹60 to a fuller spread at ₹350 — Sayan keeps Bengali catering reachable for the families who want it most.
