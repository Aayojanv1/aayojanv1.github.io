The kitchen

Bengali kitchens, women-led.

T

Tamasree Das runs Tulika's Catering Service with the quiet confidence of a Bengali kitchen that's served decades of weddings, Annaprasans, Bhai Photas, and house parties.

Bengali is the heart of every menu — doi maach, kosha mangsho, mochar ghonto, payesh done the way her family always did. But guests today expect range, so Tulika's adds Mughlai for the bold eaters and Chinese for the younger crowd, without losing the Bengali core.
