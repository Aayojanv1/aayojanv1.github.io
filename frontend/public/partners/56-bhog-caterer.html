The kitchen

Built for every kind of event.

৫৬

"56 Bhog" carries the weight of the great tradition — fifty-six dishes offered to Lord Krishna at Govardhan, a mahabhog of abundance and care. Krishanu Ghosh's kitchen draws from that lineage.

From a 50-guest birthday at home to a 5,000-guest wedding at a banquet — same kitchen, same standard, scaled to fit. Bengali warmth at the core, Mughlai and Chinese on the wings, Continental when the brief calls for it.
