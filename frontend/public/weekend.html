Weekend FAQs

Things people WhatsApp us first.

Can you arrange catering for tonight or tomorrow?+

During pre-launch our team routes weekend enquiries personally — same day in most cases. WhatsApp ASAP and we'll tell you honestly which partner has availability for your window. Smaller home events (25-80 guests) are usually doable on short notice; large weddings need more lead time.

Which areas do you serve?+

Newtown (700156, 700157, 700161 — all Action Areas), Salt Lake (700091, 700105, 700064, 700107 — Sector V, FD/GD Blocks, Karunamoyee, City Centre), and Rajarhat (700135, 700136 — Chinar Park, Tegharia, Akankha). 56 Bhog Caterer also covers central Kolkata.

How much does a Sunday lunch cost?+

Bengali home-style Sunday lunch: ₹300-₹450 per plate for 25-75 guests. Festive spreads (mutton + fish + sweets): ₹450-₹600/plate. Premium (Hilsa, Chingri Malaikari): ₹600+/plate. Final quote comes directly from the matched partner.

What's the minimum guest count?+

25 plates for most events. Some partners can do 15-20 for intimate Annaprasan or family gatherings. Tell us your number and we'll match a partner who handles your scale.

Is Aayojan really free for customers?+

Yes — 100% free. We earn from the partner caterer only when a booking confirms. You pay the caterer directly for the catering itself; Aayojan never takes money from you.

What does "FSSAI Applied" mean?+

The caterer has applied for FSSAI registration but hasn't received the license number yet. We display compliance status honestly: 2 of our partners (Curry-কথা · 56 Bhog) have verified FSSAI numbers visible above; 3 have applications in process and will display numbers as soon as issued.
