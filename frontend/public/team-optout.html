This page sets a small local flag in your browser so the Aayojan analytics treats you as internal traffic. The flag stays in this browser only — it does not sync across devices. To exclude another device or browser, visit this page on each one.

The exclusion does not block Google Analytics from loading globally — it only flips a flag (ga_opt_out=1) that the site checks before sending events.
