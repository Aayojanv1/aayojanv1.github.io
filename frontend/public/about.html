What We Stand For

Our mission & values

We're building a platform where both customers and caterers come away better off every time.

🤝

Partner-First

Caterers are not suppliers — they're our partners. We only succeed when they grow. Every product decision is filtered through this lens.

🔍

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no buried clauses. Customers know exactly what they're getting; partners know exactly what they'll pay. Trust is non-negotiable.

⚡

Speed Over Perfection

A quote delivered in 48 hours beats a perfect quote that takes a week. We obsess over reducing friction at every step of the process.

📍

Deeply Local

We're not trying to solve catering globally. We're building something that works brilliantly for Kolkata first — then expanding thoughtfully.

🌱

Grow Together

Our commission model means we only earn when our partners earn. Aligned incentives create better outcomes for everyone on the platform.

🍱

Food is Culture

Bengali wedding food is not just a meal — it's memory. We treat every event we support as something meaningful, not transactional.
