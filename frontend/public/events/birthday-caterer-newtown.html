Birthday FAQs

Questions Newtown hosts ask.

How much does birthday catering cost?+

Kids ₹350-₹450/plate. Adults ₹450-₹550/plate. Milestone ₹600-₹700/plate.

Kids' birthdays handled?+

Yes — mini burgers, nuggets, pasta station, mocktails, ice cream counter, themed cake.

Book for 25-guest home party?+

Yes — 25 plates minimum. Most home birthdays 25-80 guests.

Surprise party — discreet?+

Yes — unmarked vehicles, coordination only with you, setup timed to surprise window.

Themed parties supported?+

Yes — Frozen, dinosaur, princess, Marvel etc. Themed plating + cake + dessert station coordinated.

How early to book?+

Standard: 1 week. Weekend Nov-Feb: 2-3 weeks. Milestone with custom menu: 4 weeks.
