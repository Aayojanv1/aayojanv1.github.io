The ceremony plate

What's on a traditional Annaprasan plate?

A Mukhe Bhaat menu is not interchangeable with a wedding menu. Every dish carries ceremony meaning — and partner kitchens know which ones to include for which family.

🍚
Payesh
পায়েস
The first taste — sweet milk-rice cooked with jaggery or sugar. The single non-negotiable item on every plate.
🥣
Kheer
ক্ষীর
Thickened sweetened milk with cardamom and nuts — pairs with payesh as the auspicious start.
🍛
Bhaat with Ghee
ঘি ভাত
Plain steamed rice with a spoon of ghee — the ceremonial centerpiece touched to the baby's lips.
🥟
Luchi / Paratha
লুচি
Hot luchis or stuffed paratha for the guests, served with the curries that follow.
🥔
Aloo Dum
আলুর দম
Niramish (no onion/garlic) potato curry — the family-friendly classic Bengali side.
🍲
Cholar Dal
ছোলার ডাল
Sweetened chana dal with coconut bits — Bengali-style, ceremony-appropriate, never spicy.
🐟
Fish Fry / Chicken Kosha
মাছ ভাজা
Optional non-veg add-on. Many families keep Annaprasan vegetarian; others add fish or chicken.
🍮
Mishti Doi & Rasgulla
মিষ্টি দই
The closing sweets. Mishti doi from local mishti shops, rasgulla freshly made.

Annaprasan FAQs

Questions Bengali families ask.

What is Annaprasan or Mukhe Bhaat?+

Annaprasan (Mukhe Bhaat in Bengali) is the traditional Hindu rice-ceremony marking a baby's first taste of solid food — typically performed around 6 months of age. The menu respects ceremony customs: payesh, kheer, plain rice with ghee.

How much does Annaprasan catering cost in Kolkata?+

₹300-₹500 per plate for 25-100 guests. Simple traditional menus from ₹300/plate. Premium spreads with mishti doi, multiple sweets and add-ons land at ₹450-₹500/plate.

What's the traditional Annaprasan menu?+

Payesh, kheer, plain rice with ghee, luchi or paratha, aloo dum (niramish), cholar dal, fish fry or chicken kosha (optional), chutney, papad, mishti doi and rasgulla. Pure-veg options always available.

Can I book for 25 guests only?+

Yes — minimum batch is 25 plates. Both intimate house gatherings and larger banquet ceremonies are common via Aayojan partners.

Do caterers serve at home?+

Yes — home delivery + self-service at ₹300-₹400/plate, or full setup with service staff at ₹400-₹550/plate. Banquet venue catering also available.

Which Kolkata areas are covered?+

All over Kolkata — including Newtown, Salt Lake, Rajarhat and beyond. All partners are Bengali-speaking, ceremony-trained kitchens.

How early should I book?+

Ideally 10-15 days ahead. For weekend or auspicious dates (Saraswati Puja week, festival season) book 3-4 weeks ahead. Last-minute (7 days) is possible but limits partner choice.
