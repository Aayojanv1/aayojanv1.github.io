Corporate menu types

What kind of office lunch do you need?

Five common formats. Final menu sheet shared by your matched partner on WhatsApp.

📦
Daily Tiffin Box
Single-portion thermal box. Rice + dal + 2 sides + dessert. Delivered to office every working day. Rotation Bengali / N. Indian / Cont / Chinese.
🍱
Conference Lunch
3-5 dish buffet for 30-150 people. Includes service staff or self-serve setup with chafing dishes.
🏛️
Town-Hall Buffet
6-8 dish multi-cuisine for 100-500 employees. Includes live counter add-ons (chaat / tandoor).
🤝
Client Dinner
Premium Bengali or multi-cuisine plated dinner at office banquet space. Service staff included.
🎯
Team Event / Offsite
Casual buffet, live grills, beverage station. Common for sales kick-off, hackathon, milestone celebrations.
🌱
Jain / Pure Veg / Vegan
No-onion-garlic, Sattvik, plant-based. Many Sector V offices need this segment served separately.
