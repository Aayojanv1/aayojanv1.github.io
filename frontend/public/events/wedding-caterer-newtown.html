Wedding Caterer FAQs

Questions Newtown couples ask.

How much does a wedding caterer cost in Newtown per plate?+

₹450/plate for standard multi-cuisine, up to ₹1200+ for luxury chef-led. Mid-range Bengali-Mughlai weddings land at ₹600-₹850/plate.

Can Newtown caterers handle 500+ guest weddings?+

Yes — Aayojan partners regularly cater 500-800 guest weddings at DLF, Hyatt, Eco Park venues. Some handle 1000+ with backup kitchens.

Which Newtown venues do you work at?+

DLF lawns, Hyatt Newtown, Eco Park, Biswa Bangla Gate, Roopkala Park, Glitz, Action Area III community halls. Anywhere in Newtown 700156/157/161.

Are live counters available?+

Yes — chaat, kebab, tandoor, sizzler, dessert, biryani, Chinese wok. Each counter adds ₹40-₹180/plate depending on cuisine.

How far ahead should I book?+

Peak season (Nov-Feb): 3-4 months ahead. Off-season: 6-8 weeks. Last-minute (2-3 weeks) possible but partner choice limited.

Can I taste before booking?+

Yes — ₹199/plate veg, ₹399/plate non-veg, 2-4 people. Arranged within 48-72 hours of enquiry.

What's a typical Bengali wedding menu?+

Welcome drinks, 2-3 starters, bhetki paturi or doi maach, mutton/chicken kosha, mughlai biryani, niramish, sukto, papad, sweets (sandesh, rasgulla, mishti doi), ice-cream counter. Continental and Chinese sections often added.

Booking a wedding in Newtown?

Date, venue, guest count, cuisine preference — share these. We route to matched verified kitchens via WhatsApp the same day.
