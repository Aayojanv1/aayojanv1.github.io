What's on a Griha Pravesh plate?

Two halves: puja-window (pure veg, niramish) and post-puja (optional non-veg). Many families do veg-only for the day.

Griha Pravesh FAQs

Questions new home-owners ask.

What is Griha Pravesh?+

The Hindu housewarming ceremony — performed when a family moves into a new home. Puja followed by a meal for family, neighbours and close friends.

How much does it cost?+

Pure veg from ₹300/plate. Veg + non-veg ₹400-₹500/plate. Premium spreads ₹500-₹600/plate.

Traditional menu?+

Veg first session: luchi, niramish, aloo dum, basanti pulao, sukto, chutney, payesh, mishti doi. Non-veg later (optional): bhetki paturi, rohu kalia, mutton kosha.

Pure-veg for puja day?+

Yes — pure-veg, niramish (no onion/garlic), Sattvik and Jain menus available.

Home setup included?+

Yes — drop-off + self-service, or full setup with service staff, chafing dishes, plates/cutlery.

How early to book?+

At least 2 weeks ahead. Peak season (Saraswati Puja, Bengali New Year, Diwali weeks): 3-4 weeks.

Hosting a Griha Pravesh?

Tell us the date, puja-window timing, veg/non-veg preference and guest count. We route to the right kitchen.
