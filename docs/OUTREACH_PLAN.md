## 📱 WhatsApp Business Setup (PRIORITY #1)

### Why Separate Business Account?
- Professional identity (not personal mobile)
- Business profile with logo, description, catalog
- Auto-replies, quick replies, labels for CRM
- Can use WhatsApp Business API later (same number)
- Builds trust — caterers see "Aayojan" not a random number

### Setup Steps:
1. **Get a dedicated SIM** (Jio/Airtel ₹149/mo plan is enough)
2. **Install WhatsApp Business app** (NOT regular WhatsApp)
3. **Configure business profile:**
   - Business Name: `Aayojan — Catering Platform`
   - Category: `Food & Beverage`
   - Description: `AI-powered catering platform for Newtown, Kolkata. We connect event organizers with the best local caterers.`
   - Address: `Newtown, Kolkata 700156`
   - Website: `https://aayojan.online`
   - Email: `hello@aayojan.online`
   - Hours: `Mon-Sat 10AM-8PM`
4. **Set auto-reply:**
   > "Thanks for reaching out to Aayojan! 🙏 We connect caterers with customers in Newtown. Reply 1 for Partner Registration, 2 for Customer Query."
5. **Create catalog:** Add 2-3 items (Full Catering, Bulk Order, Tasting Session)
6. **Set away message** for after hours

### Cost: ₹149/mo (SIM) + ₹0 (WhatsApp Business is free)

## 💡 Tips for WhatsApp Outreach

1. **Send during business hours** (10AM-6PM) — higher response rate
2. **Personalize** — mention their business name, not generic blast
3. **Don't spam** — max 10-15 new contacts/day to avoid WhatsApp ban
4. **Use labels** in WhatsApp Business:
   - 🟢 Onboarded
   - 🟡 Interested (follow up)
   - 🔴 Not interested
   - ⚪ No response
5. **Save broadcast lists** for future updates to all partners
6. **Send voice notes** occasionally — feels more personal than text
